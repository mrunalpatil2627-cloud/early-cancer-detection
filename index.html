Early detection methods for the most common diagnoses.

Breast Cancer

Monthly self-exams and annual mammograms for those over 40 are the primary defense.

02

Colorectal

Screening via colonoscopy or stool-based tests should typically begin at age 45.

03

Lung Cancer

Annual low-dose CT scans are recommended for long-term smokers between 50-80.
